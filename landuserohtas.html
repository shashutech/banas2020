Rohtas Landuse

As per the record of Department of Agriculture, Government of Bihar (2009-10), the forest covers of the district is 66723 ha. The total copped area is 23870 ha whereas the total nonagricultural land is 152052 ha. Agriculture is the main livehood of the population. The agriculture calendar starts from the month of July and continued to the month of June of the succeeding year before the onset of monsoon every year. Thus a calendar year is divided into four agriculture season viz., Bhadai, Aghani Rabi and Garma.
