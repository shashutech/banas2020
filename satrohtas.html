Rohtas Satellite View

Rohtas occupies an area of 3851 Sq Km. Rohtas district extends between N. latitude 24°29‟ and 25°22‟40” and E. longitudes 83°19‟ and 84°29‟ and falls in Survey of India Degree Sheet No. 63O, 63P, 72C, and 72D. It bounded on the north by the district of Buxer and Bhojpur on the South by the district of Palamu&Garhwa of Jharkhand State on the east, by the district of Aurangabad and part of Gaya district and on the west, the district of Kaimur (Bhabhua). The river Sone forms the southern and eastern boundary of the district.
