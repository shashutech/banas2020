Rohtas Administration

Rohtas district is one of the thirty-eight districts of Bihar state, India. Administrative headquarter of the district is Sasaram.Rohtas district is divided into 19 community development blocks, which are grouped together into 3 sub-divisions based at Sasaram, Bikramganj, and Dehri, respectively.The 19 CD Blocks are as follows:- KOCHAS, DINARA, DAWATH, SURYAPURA, BIKRAMGANJ, KARAKAT, NASRIGANJ, RAJPUR, SANJHAULI, NOKHA, KARGAHAR, CHENARI, NAUHATTA, SHEOSAGAR, SASARAM, AKORHI, GOLA, DEHRI, TILAUTHU, ROHTAS
