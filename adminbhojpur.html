Bhojpur Administration

Bhojpur district is one of the thirty-eight districts of Bihar state in northern India. Arrah is the administrative headquarters of this district.Bhojpur district has three 3 Sub-Divisions ie Ara Sadar, Jagdishpur and Piro which is further divided into 14 Blocks These 14 Blocks consists of 228 Gram Panchayats and 1,244 Villages.These are 14 blocks of Bhojpur District:-ARRAH,AGIAON,BARHARA,KOILWAR,UDWANTNAGAR,SANDESH,SAHAR,GARHANI,PIRO,CHARPOKHARI,TARARI,JAGDISPUR,BIHIYA,SHAHPUR
