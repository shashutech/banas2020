SONE COMMAND AREA:-

Sone Command Area Development Authority was created on 1st November, 1973 for integrated all round development of the command particularly for the purpose of increasing the utility of irrigation potential and agricultural output. Thus some of the aims of Sane Command Area Development Agency are maximum utilization of available water of Sane canal system for increasing agriculture production, optimum utilization of underground water, provision of drainage, maintenance and regulation of distribution system, restoration of proper cRP programme, production management and increasing production and providing marketing facilities.

In the beginning, only five districts of Patna and Magadh divisionsnamely Patna, Bhojpur, Rohtas, Aurangabad and Gaya were kept under Sone command Area. In the course when district of Gaya was bifurcated and new district of Jahanabad was created, then the total districts under this command rose to six. Later on Buxar district was created out of Bhojpur and Bhabhua was carved out of Rohtas district during reorganization of districts. Thus altogether eight districts came under the area of this command and we are going to do mapping of 4 ditricts :- BHOJPUR, BUXAR, KAIMUR, ROHTAS
