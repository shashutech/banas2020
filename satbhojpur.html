Bhojpur Satellite View

Bhojpur is located at 25010’-25040’ North Latitude & 83045’-84045’ East Longitude. It has an average elevation of 72 meters (236 feet). Bhojpur is surrounded by the state of Uttar Pradesh & Saran district in the north, Rohtas & Aurangabad districts in the south, Patna in the east, and Buxar district in the west
